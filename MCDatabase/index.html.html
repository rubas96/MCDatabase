How it works

Quick start: Go to Collection and check the boxes for the products you own (Core Set, hero packs, big boxes…). Then the Heroes, Cards, and Deck Builder tabs only show what you actually have.

Where the data comes from

Card data is the open MarvelCDB dataset. The first time you open the app (and whenever you press ↻ Update data) it downloads the latest cards — so when a new expansion is released, just press Update and the new product appears in your Collection automatically. Card images load from MarvelCDB when you're online.

Internet & offline

You need internet the first time (to download the card database and the SQLite engine) and when you press Update. After that, your data is stored on this computer and the app keeps working offline — though card images need a connection.

Your data is yours

Your collection and decks are saved locally in this browser. Use ⭳ Backup to save a JSON file (keep it safe / move it between computers) and ⭱ Restore to load it back. The whole card database is real SQLite — you can also export it from here:

Deck-building rules checked

A legal deck = your hero's signature cards (added automatically) + cards from one aspect + Basic cards, at least 40 cards total, respecting each card's copy limit and how many copies you own. Most decks run 40–50 cards. Deadpool and a few special heroes work differently — the app will still let you build, but double-check those manually.
